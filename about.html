About Me

Who I am, where I come from and what I do?

Background

Born and raised in beautiful Southern California by my Spanish and French parents Natacha and Nicolas. I was taught 3 languages at once and have always been fluent. As a young boy I enjoyed Surfing, playing tennis and 3D modeling. My passion for tennis lead me to being a top ranked player in the United States and a Cif double champion my senior year. I went to the San Diego French American School for middle school and attended La Jolla Country Day School for high school. My passion for 3D modeling led me to create BrewStop with a colleague of mine Henry Timmermann. Being one that loved math and Spanish culture I decided to apply to various colleges in Spain. I am currently a second year BBA + Data Analytics student at the Universidad de Navarra in Spain.
