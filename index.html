    Projects I Have Worked On

    A short summary about the projects I have worked on throughout my career

    Statistics project in R about time spent, relating data to current and past events. The project involves analyzing, manipulating, and visualizing data for a comprehensive understanding of time. It utilizes tools learned through classes and past projects. The analysis was conducted entirely using the R programming language, creating visuals and manipulating data to extract a complete analysis.

    Contributed to AIM2Flourish by conducting research and interviews to showcase businesses aligning with UN SDGs. Authored impactful stories that emphasize the link between business success and sustainable development. Inspired and educated peers through effective communication on the AIM2Flourish platform, playing a key role in recognizing businesses contributing to positive societal and environmental change.

    BrewStop

    Led the 3D modeling team and guided product development, concurrently filing a utility patent. During my tenure at Brewstop, I attained advanced proficiency in Onshape and acquired skills in SOLIDWORKS, enabling the creation of intricate 3-Dimensional models for our products. Actively engaged in data collection through rigorous testing, I presented our products at numerous events to diverse audiences. These experiences have fueled my passion for 3D modeling, leading me to continue honing my skills in this area during my spare time.
